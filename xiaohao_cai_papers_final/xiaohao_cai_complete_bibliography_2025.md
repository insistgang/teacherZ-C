## üéì Key Methodological Contributions

### **SaT (Smoothing and Thresholding) Framework**
- **Innovation**: Novel paradigm for image segmentation
- **Core Idea**: Segmentation = Restoration + Thresholding
- **Variants**: 
  - T-ROF: Thresholded Rudin-Osher-Fatemi
  - SLaT: Smoothing, Lifting, and Thresholding
- **Applications**: Grayscale, color, noisy, blurry images
- **Advantages**: Fast, flexible, supports arbitrary phases

### **Tight-Frame Methods**
- **Applications**: Vessel segmentation in medical imaging
- **Innovation**: Wavelet-based segmentation on sphere
- **Features**: Handles anisotropic structures, spherical data
- **Key Papers**: 2013 SIAM J. Imaging Sci. paper

### **Graph-Based Methods**
- **3D Tree Delineation**: Multi-class graph cut (MCGC)
- **Applications**: Forest monitoring, biomass estimation
- **Innovation**: Integrates LiDAR and hyperspectral data
- **Key Papers**: 2017 Remote Sensing of Environment

### **Radio Astronomy**
- **Online Interferometric Imaging**: Real-time processing
- **Uncertainty Quantification**: Proximal MCMC, MAP estimation
- **Big Data Ready**: Scales to SKA-scale observations
- **Key Papers**: 2017-2018 MNRAS series

### **Machine Learning**
- **Few-shot Learning**: Non-negative subspace methods
- **Tensor Decomposition**: Tucker approximation, CUR decomposition
- **Personality Detection**: Multi-modal approaches
- **Medical Imaging**: MRI reconstruction, fake news detection
